Each project includes one (1) round of revisions at no extra charge, used between the first draft and the final draft. A "round" means one consolidated set of feedback, sent in a single message, covering all images or files in the project.

Once you approve the final draft and pay the 30% balance, the project is considered closed. No further revisions are included after this point.

Additional revisions, whether before or after final approval, are chargeable at our standard revision rate, which will be quoted before any further work begins.

Revision requests must be sent within 7 days of receiving the first draft. After 7 days without feedback, the first draft will be treated as the final draft and the 30% balance will become payable.

Revisions are limited to refinements within the original scope (e.g. adjusting brightness, swapping a furniture piece, tweaking a chibi expression). Substantial changes — such as restaging an entire room with new furniture style, recreating a floor plan from new measurements, or redesigning a chibi from scratch — are treated as a new project and quoted separately.
